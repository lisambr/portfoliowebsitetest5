THE ASSIGNMENT
Create a story without words filmed by your smartphone device. Edit the footage on PremierePro and make it no longer than 2 minutes.

MY TAKE
In this project time was of the essence. We only had half a week and needed to think, produce and present fast. It was very exciting to push the limits of what was possible under pressure. Furthermore we were discovering everything about filming and editing with a new team which also taught me about my own boundaries.
