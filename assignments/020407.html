THE ASSIGNMENT
THE ASSIGNMENT: During this task we were put into groups and asked to make an interactive movie poster for the film ET.

MY TAKE
This was our first group work and probably our longest group-work. It mixed design and coding and pushed every person in our team to go beyond the limit. I was in charge of all the visuals and really kept on discovering in which ways to use illustrator. Visuals became more and more apparent to me as an interesting path.
