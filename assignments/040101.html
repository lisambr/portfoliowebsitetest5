THE ASSIGNMENT
THE ASSIGNMENT: During this week we did our first Design Sprint around the context of redesigning a mobile equivalent of Fronter.

MY TAKE
During this group work we learned a lot about the sprint and even more so about testing, understanding the usability of features and realising that some concepts simply do not work even though in theory they seemed so great. At the end it is the user who is king and not the person who designs.
