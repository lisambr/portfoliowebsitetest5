THE ASSIGNMENT
This was a “Project-Pool” assignment. We were working in pairs to create an animation out of a list of examples. On the first day I chose a “kill the fly” game even though you might notice some alterations.

MY TAKE
During this exercise I quickly realised I could add elements from previous assignments to make it more dynamic and interesting. I replaced the fly with my bird and used sprite sheet so it would be even more fun to play around with. I did realize that the code became WAY more confusing this way and that the dead-bird had to be a sprite-sheet too.
