THE ASSIGNMENT
Create a bicycle guide for non Danes. This included an XD prototype, a coded website and a Video.

MY TAKE
This assignment was highly challenging as it had so many elements (design, filming, acting, editing, coding and creating content). It was a hard job to stay on top of it all and also to follow other peoples designs instead of my own vision. I had to let go of some of my ideas and trust the process.
